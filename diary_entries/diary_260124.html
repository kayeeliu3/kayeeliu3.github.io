It is currently 1:37am on Friday 26th January 2024, and I believe I just had one of the most extraordinary, if not the most extraordinary, night of my life. To be able to meet such incredibly talented and prolific musicians and singers, and to be able to be a part of it - I will never forget the intoxicating harmonies and joyous rhythms that struck my heart in an angle that had never been touched in such a delightful manner. The ambience with the slightly dim yellow lights and the vibes of a tavern, along with the bustle of the jigs and the flow of the guitar melodies - it is not a night I ever wish to forget. I look up to the vigour and contagious energy surrounding the buoyant fiddler, and I can only hope that I will be able to feel such an emotion once more.
