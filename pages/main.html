current song obsession:
醉醉渔，唱唱晚
[eng: evening song of the drunken fishermen]
(Performed by Zi De Guqin Studio)
such a beautiful song, with a harmonious and swaying feel. a fairly reserved, hushed starting duet of the guzheng and guqin, later woven alongside a flute, before the dances of the fishermen as they drink into the sunset at 2:28. the udu is fantastic here, along with flickering arpeggios and lively runs on the flute. notice how well the zithers lay the grounds and steering of the song, and the small cymbals giving a soft highlight to the rhythms. goosebumps everytime to me at this point of the song.

if you can, please watch the original here - the whole video resembles a moving painting.
